How it started

The tree grove started out as a little idea me and a friend had. To make a place that was filled with storys and adventures that every one could enjoy. We started small with selling books a local book fairs and slowly saved up to own a book store.

We started off with only 50 books, and now we have over 2000 books read to go and be read.

Our goal

Our goal is by the end of the year to have sold out of books at least once.
